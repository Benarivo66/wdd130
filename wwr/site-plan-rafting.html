Overview

Purpose

To create a platform to market and promote my rafting services to a wider audience

Audience

Our target audience for the water rafting website primarily consists of adventure enthusiasts and outdoor seekers who are eager to explore the excitement of whitewater rafting. What do they want? They are in search of heart-pounding excitement and the challenge of navigating wild river rapids. What are their needs that aren't being met?They desire tailored experiences to cater to varying skill levels and group sizes. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? For now users will be accessing the websites through their smart phones and computers.
